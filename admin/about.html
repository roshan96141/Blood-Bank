ABOUT US

The Blood Bank was started in the year 2016. Till 2017 March, the task of the Blood Bank was limited to collection of whole blood and distribution to the needy patients. Since 1st April 2017, Blood Bank has been granted license to segregate blood components.The collection of blood units has increased from few thousands in the formative years to more than 25,000 units during 2017-18. The Blood bank is a designated Major blood Bank. The Blood Bank has also entered into Memorandum of understanding with many private and rural hospitals to act as Mother blood bank to their Blood Storage Centers. The Blood Bank is also encouraging the district branches of Indian Red Cross Society to have their own blood storage centres and blood bank.
